Get to Know About Me!

Theona Arlinton, a dedicated Computer Science major at BINUS University, is not just a tech enthusiast but also a versatile artist with a passion for illustration. Her artistic journey is deeply intertwined with her academic pursuits, creating a unique blend of technology and creativity.


Theona's portfolio in art reflects her proficiency in the realm of illustration, where she demonstrates a keen eye for detail and a distinctive style. Her artistic endeavors go beyond the conventional boundaries, showcasing a fusion of traditional and digital techniques that captivate the viewer's imagination.


What sets Theona apart is her commitment to freelancing, particularly in the realm of manhua illustration. The intricacies of this specialized form require both artistic finesse and a deep understanding of storytelling, a challenge she wholeheartedly embraces.


Through her freelance work, Theona not only hones her artistic skills but also contributes to the vibrant world of manhua, bringing characters and narratives to life. Her dedication to this craft is evident in each stroke of her digital brush, creating visuals that resonate with emotion and storytelling.


As Theona continues to pursue her Computer Science degree, she remains committed to expanding her artistic horizons. Her portfolio is a testament to the seamless integration of technology and artistry, showcasing the limitless possibilities when one combines a passion for illustration with a foundation in computer science.
